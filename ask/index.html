Example answers

What a good answer looks like

Short previews — your real answer adapts to your hotel, timing, and group.

  • Question We’re staying at La Cantera with kids and need dinner tonight.

    Answer preview Stay nearby if timing is tight. Go downtown only if you have enough energy for parking, walking, and the ride back. Start with a resort-area dinner, then save River Walk for a planned downtown night.

  • Question We have one night downtown. What should we do first?

    Answer preview Start with the River Walk before peak dinner crowds, then choose one anchor: Alamo photos, Tower views, Market Square, or a better dinner path based on where you’re staying.

  • Question I have a flight later. Where should we eat?

    Answer preview Stay realistic. Choose food based on your airport timing, luggage situation, and traffic window instead of chasing a spot too far across town.
