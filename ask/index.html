River Walk timing guide

The River Walk hits differently depending on when you go. Earlier evening is better for easier walking and photos. Later evening brings more lights, more energy, and more crowds. Pick the timing that matches your night.

  • Early evening = easier pace
  • Later evening = more energy
  • Best for dinner, drinks, and walking
