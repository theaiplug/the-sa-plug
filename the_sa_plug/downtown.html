How to route downtown without over-planning

San Antonio rewards one anchor neighborhood at a time. Stack the Pearl, Hemisfair, or Market Square as second acts — not simultaneous detours.

  • Dinner reservations first on busy weekends and convention weeks.
  • Bookend summer days with shade, indoor pauses, and shorter outdoor legs.
  • Barge cruises work best mid-route for orientation — not squeezed in at closing time.
  • Prefer rideshare pins one block off the busiest bridge pinch points.
