THE REALITY

Most websites lose the lead after the click

Customers land on your site with questions about pricing, timing, availability, booking, service details, or whether they can trust you. If the site only says “contact us,” the lead gets weaker.

Missed calls

High call volume and after-hours traffic turn into voicemail instead of booked work.

Slow follow-up

Leads sit while someone chases details across text, email, and sticky notes.

Messy contact forms

One-line messages force five reply emails before you can quote the job.

Repeated customer questions

Staff answers the same FAQ loop instead of moving people to the next step.

No booking path

Interested customers bounce because there is no clear appointment or request route.

No owner visibility

You cannot see what came in, from where, or what still needs a reply.
