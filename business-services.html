THE LEAD LEAK

Most websites get the visit — then lose the customer.

Your website should not just collect a name. It should answer first questions, capture the right details, route the request, and show the owner what needs action.

  1. 1

    Missed calls

    After-hours traffic turns into voicemail instead of booked work.

  2. 2

    Weak forms

    One-line messages force extra follow-up before you can quote.

  3. 3

    Slow follow-up

    Good leads sit while someone chases details across texts and inboxes.

  4. 4

    Repeated questions

    Your team answers the same FAQ loop instead of moving people forward.

  5. 5

    No booking or request path

    Interested customers bounce because the next step is unclear.

  6. 6

    No owner visibility

    You cannot see what came in, where it came from, or what still needs a reply.
