First time in San Antonio? Here’s the move.

Use this as the quick-start route: what you pass coming in from the airport, what to see first, and how to choose your first dinner.

Airport → Zoo corridor

San Antonio Zoo, Japanese Tea Garden, Brackenridge Park, Witte Museum, and The DoSeum sit along one of the best family and daytime corridors.

Pearl + Hotel Emma

Great for dinner, drinks, couples, business travelers, and visitors who want a polished local-feeling night.

Downtown + River Walk

Take the river barge cruise first if you can. It gives you the lay of the land fast.

Market Square + Tower

Market Square and Mi Tierra give you culture and color. Tower of the Americas gives you the view and dinner-at-the-top option.

Willie note: If it’s your first time, I usually recommend the River Walk barge cruise first. After that, choose between classic downtown, Pearl, Market Square, or Tower depending on your group.
