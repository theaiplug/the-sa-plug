Resort food guide

What’s good to eat near the resort?

JW Marriott, La Cantera, Hyatt Hill Country, The Rim, Stone Oak, SeaWorld, and nearby dinner ideas.

The resort zones are often 20–25 minutes from downtown, so the first question is: easy nearby or worth the ride?

JW Marriott / Stone Oak / 281

Easy: 18 Oaks, Cibolo Moon, FIAMMÉ, High Velocity.

Nearby: J-Prime, Chama Gaúcha, La Panadería, Lupe Tortilla, Parry’s.

Driver note: JW is a resort bubble. Ask if they want easy or worth-the-ride.

La Cantera / The Rim / Fiesta Texas

Approved: Brasão, J-Prime, Parry’s, La Panadería, Lupe Tortilla.

Research: Signature, Perry’s, Haywire, Whiskey Cake, Palenque Grill, Paesanos 1604, Silo.

Driver note: one of the easier resort areas for food.

Hyatt Hill Country / SeaWorld

Easy: Antlers Lodge, Springhouse Café, Charlie’s Long Bar.

Nearby: Fish City Grill, The Jerk Shack, Via 313, BJ’s, Alamo Ranch options.

Driver note: family/SeaWorld zone first. For serious dinner, ride farther.
