Summer family guide

Families coming in for summer need a simple plan.

SeaWorld, Fiesta Texas, downtown with kids, the zoo corridor, caverns, and resort-pool logic.

Theme parks

SeaWorld for Hyatt / west-side families. Six Flags Fiesta Texas for La Cantera / Rim guests.

Downtown with kids

River barge cruise, Hemisfair, Tower of the Americas, Market Square, and the Alamo during the day.

Zoo + museum corridor

Zoo, Japanese Tea Garden, Brackenridge Park, Witte Museum, and The DoSeum.

Natural Bridge Caverns

A strong family half-day memory maker when they want something outside downtown.

Resort family rule

If they paid for a resort, use the resort. Add one strong off-property activity, not five.

Before flight with kids

Keep it simple: breakfast, one nearby attraction, or a short stop that does not risk missing the flight.
