Where to eat

Willie Approved food picks by situation.

Use this when visitors ask for real Tex-Mex, steak, BBQ, Italian, seafood, breakfast, Pearl, or drinks.

Real Tex-Mex / Mexican

Soluna is the favorite — ribeye tacos and strong chispas. Also: La Fonda, La Fogata, Rosario’s. Downtown: Domingo or Ácenar.

Do not recommend: Casa Rio.

Steakhouse

Bohanan’s downtown. J-Prime for north-side upscale. Ruth’s Chris for familiar business-safe steakhouse.

BBQ

Pinkerton’s for downtown visitors. 2M, Reese Bros, and Burnt Bean stay as research/serious BBQ candidates.

Italian

Paesanos on the River Walk. Nonna Osteria and Battalion are research picks for downtown/Southtown Italian.

Brazilian steakhouse

Brasão first, Chama Gaúcha second, Fogo de Chão third.

Seafood / ceviche

El Bucanero is the top ceviche pick. El Cevichero is second. Leche de Tigre is real good also.

Pearl

Brasserie, Southerleigh, Cured, Best Quality Daughter, Isidore, Pullman Market, Bakery Lorraine, Amelia Social Lounge.

Drinks

Esquire, 1Watson, Tenfold, Mad Dogs, Amelia Social Lounge. Hotel Emma/Sternewirth is a needs-visit pick.

Breakfast

La Panadería and Alamo Biscuit. Mi Tierra for late-night cultural food.
