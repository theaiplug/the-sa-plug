Downtown guide

Downtown, River Walk, Convention Center, and one-night plans.

For convention guests, downtown hotel visitors, and anyone trying to make the right move without wasting the night.

First move

River Walk barge cruise. It’s one of the easiest ways to understand downtown quickly.

Classic stops

Alamo during the day, Tower of the Americas, Market Square, Mi Tierra, and Spurs games during season.

Downtown food anchors

Bohanan’s for steak, Paesanos for River Walk Italian, Pinkerton’s for BBQ, Domingo or Ácenar for downtown Mexican.

Business dinner

Bohanan’s, Paesanos, Biga, Nonna, or Pearl if they’ll ride.

Drinks

Esquire Tavern, 1Watson, Tenfold, or Mad Dogs for bar-hopping.

Late night

Mi Tierra when they want a memorable cultural late-night stop.

Breakfast

La Panadería or Alamo Biscuit.
