## Master rule

**Price real deliverables first. Package names come second. AI language comes third.**

| Order | Question |
|-------|----------|
| 1 | What does the client actually want? |
| 2 | What does the client call the problem? |
| 3 | What real deliverables are included? |
| 4 | What tools, integrations, and pass-through costs apply? |
| 5 | How much setup/build work is required? |
| 6 | What needs ongoing monthly management? |
| 7 | What approval, safety, or compliance burden exists? |
| 8 | Should this be custom quoted? |

Only after those answers: name the package, then explain where AI assists production (not where AI “runs the business”).

## Global custom-quote triggers

Always custom quote (no standard lock range without owner sign-off):

- Multi-location or franchise dashboards
- Complex CRM/ERP integrations (Salesforce, ServiceTitan, etc.)
- AI voice **implementation** (beyond planning)
- Advanced public AI chat at scale
- Regulated or advice-adjacent industries (legal, medical, tax, financial, insurance)
- High ad spend accounts (typically **$10,000+/mo** spend — confirm threshold per deal)
- Very high content volume (daily multi-platform publishing)
- Advanced reporting / BI / custom apps
- Payment, invoice, or listing automation
- Anything where wrong copy creates legal, safety, or professional liability
