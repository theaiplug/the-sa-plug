## 3. Current master offer language

### The simple definition

The AI Plug helps businesses apply practical AI to the customer path behind the business.

That includes the path from attention to action:

1. A customer finds the business.
2. The business explains what it does.
3. The customer asks a question or takes action.
4. The system captures the right details.
5. The right person sees the request.
6. Follow-up happens.
7. The owner can see what needs attention.
8. The system improves over time.

### Plain-English version

The AI Plug helps businesses stop losing opportunities because marketing, content, customer questions, intake, follow-up, and internal work are not connected.

### Best public phrasing

The AI Plug helps businesses move faster, respond better, publish more consistently, and build systems that keep improving.
