## 1. Purpose

This file defines how the Owner Command Dashboard should be updated to match the current live The AI Plug business direction.

The dashboard should no longer treat business leads as generic “website,” “form,” “lead,” or “automation” requests.

The current live business direction positions The AI Plug as a **Practical AI Adoption + Growth Systems** company.

The dashboard should help the owner/operator:

1. understand what kind of business submitted the request
2. identify where opportunities are leaking
3. recommend the safest first AI system
4. map the request to one or more service lanes
5. generate field-ready discovery questions
6. generate plain-English pitch language
7. handle objections safely
8. recommend a pricing range
9. prepare a proposal summary
10. track follow-up, close, onboarding, delivery, and monthly improvement

This is a dashboard update plan, not a Cursor implementation prompt yet.

## 11. Proposal Builder update

The proposal builder should stop producing generic menu-style proposals.

It should build a proposal from diagnosis.

### Proposal output structure

1. Business problem diagnosed
2. Primary leak
3. Recommended first system
4. Why this is the best first move
5. Scope included
6. Scope excluded
7. Client responsibilities
8. Approval checkpoints
9. Setup price range
10. Monthly option
11. Safe guardrails
12. Next step

### Proposal language style

Plain English. Owner-facing. No jargon.

### Proposal template

**Problem diagnosed**  
Based on what you described, the main issue is not just needing more tools. The issue is that [calls/forms/questions/follow-up/content/customer requests] are not connected clearly enough.

**Recommended first system**  
The safest first move is [recommended system]. This focuses on [specific leak] before building a larger system.

**Why this first**  
This keeps the project manageable, gives your team something useful to review, and helps us prove the workflow before expanding.

**Included**  
[scope bullets]

**Not included**  
[exclusions]

**Price range**  
Based on the current scope, this would usually fall between [range].

**Next step**  
The next step is a short discovery review so we can confirm the request path, approvals, and first build phase.

## 12. Discovery Script Builder update

The dashboard should generate discovery questions based on business type + leak + first system.

### Universal discovery questions

- Where do customers first discover you?
- What action do you want them to take?
- Where do they get stuck?
- What information do you need before responding?
- Who receives the request today?
- How do you follow up?
- What does the owner wish they could see?
- Who approves public-facing language?
- What would be easier 30 days after launch?

### Service-lane question examples

#### AI Marketing Systems
- Where do most new customers find you now?
- Are you currently running Google Ads or paid social?
- What content do you wish you published consistently?
- What service/category/location pages are missing?
- What happens after someone clicks your ad or finds your page?

#### AI Customer Experience
- What questions do customers ask over and over?
- What answers are safe to approve?
- What questions should always go to a person?
- Where should a customer go after the answer?
- What handoff should happen?

#### AI Intake + Automation
- What information do you wish customers gave you upfront?
- Who receives the request today?
- What details are usually missing?
- What should happen after the request is submitted?
- What follow-up is safe and repeatable?

#### AI Business Assistants
- What internal work gets repeated every week?
- What does the owner or team have to summarize, draft, research, or organize?
- What decisions should stay human?
- What templates or SOPs would help?

#### AI Growth Management
- What needs to be updated monthly?
- Who currently watches the website, content, forms, ads, and workflows?
- What would make monthly support valuable?
- What should be tested next?

## 13. Objection Handler update

The dashboard should generate objection replies based on the new direction.

### Universal objections

- I already have a website.
- I do not need AI.
- Can you guarantee leads?
- Is this just ChatGPT?
- Can it replace staff?
- This sounds expensive.
- I need to think about it.
- Will this rank me on Google?
- Can it send messages automatically?
- What if the AI says something wrong?
- I already have someone who handles my website.
- I use Facebook / Instagram instead of a website.
- I get enough business already.
- We tried automation before and it did not work.
- I do not have time to manage another tool.
- Send me information.
- Call me later.
- Can you do this for my industry?

### Response formula

1. Acknowledge the concern.
2. Reframe in plain English.
3. State the safe truth.
4. Connect back to the business leak.
5. Offer the smallest next step.

### Required dashboard output

- short response
- longer response
- bridge question
- do-not-say warnings
